A renowned airline desires a feature-rich flight booking system to optimize flight reservations and passenger management. For each flight, there should be flight number, departure and arrival times, and seat availability. Reservations should be associated with passenger and flight, each reservation identified by reservation ID and containing details like check-in and check-out dates, and passenger profile. Furthermore, passenger should have their own profiles with passenger ID, name, contact information, and order history. The system should empower passengers to search for available flights, book tickets, select preferred seats, and complete secure payments. The system should also facilitate reservation management and provide ticket modification capabilities.
